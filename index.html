Booking

Booking is the act of reserving or securing something for future use. This can be anything from a movie ticket to a hotel room to a flight. Bookings are usually made in advance, and they can be confirmed with a payment or deposit. Once a booking is confirmed, the person who made the booking has the right to use the service or item at the agreed-upon time.

Pricings

Pricing is the process of setting the price of a good or service. It is one of the most important decisions a business makes, as it can affect everything from profitability to market share. There are many factors to consider when setting prices, such as the cost of production, the competition, and the target market. Businesses must also decide whether to use a cost-based, value-based, or competition-based pricing strategy.
